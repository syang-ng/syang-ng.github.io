About Me

I am a fourth-year PhD candidate in the Computer Science Department at Yale University. I am very fortunate to be advised by Prof. Fan Zhang. Previously, I obtained my Bachelor’s and Master’s degrees from Fudan University.

My research focuses on analyzing potential issues in decentralized systems, especially those related to Maximal Extractable Value (MEV). Feel free to reach out if you are interested in my work or in collaborating with me!
